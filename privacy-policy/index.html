EventsNPeeps Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 31st, 2026

1. Introduction:

This Privacy Policy explains how Thirty Seven Labs Inc. (“Company”, “we”, “us”, or “our”) collects, uses, and protects your information when you use the EventsNPeeps platform, including: • the EventsNPeeps website • mobile applications • related services (the “Service”). By using the Service you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect:

Information You Provide When you create an account or use the Service we may collect: • name • email address • phone number • password • profile information • photos • interests and preferences • messages and communications sent through the Service • event descriptions and comments. We may receive account-related information from authentication providers used to access the Service, such as name, email address, and profile information.
2.1. Activity and Usage Information: We may collect information about how you interact with the Service, such as: • pages visited • events joined • groups joined • features used • time spent on the platform.
2.2. Device Information: We may automatically collect: • device type • operating system • browser type • IP address • device identifiers.
2.3. Location Information: With your permission we may collect approximate or precise location data to help show relevant events near you.
2.4. Cookies and Tracking Technologies: We may use cookies and similar technologies to: • remember user preferences • improve functionality • analyze usage patterns • enhance security.
2.5. Payment Information: Paid memberships and subscriptions may be processed through third-party payment providers depending on platform. On Android devices, payments may be processed through Google Play Billing. On iOS devices, payments may be processed through Apple in-app purchase systems. On web platforms, payments may be processed through providers such as Stripe. We do not store full payment card information on our servers.

3. How We Use Information:

We may use collected information to: • authenticate users and manage accounts • provide and maintain the Service • personalize user experiences • facilitate events and connections • improve platform functionality • communicate with users • detect fraud and abuse • comply with legal obligations.
3.1 Push Notifications: We may send notifications related to events, messages, subscriptions, platform updates, and account activity. Notification preferences may be adjusted in device settings.

4. Subscription and Billing Information:

• Subscription and billing information associated with memberships may be processed through third-party payment providers. • We may receive limited information such as subscription status, transaction identifiers, payment confirmations, and billing metadata.

5. Sharing of Information:

We may share information with: • authentication providers • service providers supporting the platform • payment processors • analytics providers • legal authorities when required by law. We use third-party service providers to help operate the Service. These providers may process information on our behalf for hosting, authentication, analytics, payments, communications, notifications, storage, and infrastructure services. Examples of providers may include cloud and infrastructure providers, authentication providers, payment processors, and analytics services. We do not sell user personal data.
5.1 Public Profile/Event Visibility: Certain profile information, events, groups, messages, and content you share through the Service may be visible to other users depending on your settings and use of the Service.

6. Data Retention:

We retain personal information only as long as necessary to: • operate the Service • comply with legal obligations • resolve disputes • enforce agreements. Users may request account deletion through the app profile settings or by contacting support@EventsNPeeps.com. We will process account deletion requests within a reasonable timeframe, subject to legal, security, fraud prevention, or operational requirements. Certain information may be retained for fraud prevention, legal compliance, security purposes, dispute resolution, or enforcement of our agreements.

7. Data Security:

We implement reasonable security measures including: • encrypted connections (TLS) • access controls • monitoring and logging • regular backups. However, no system can guarantee absolute security.

8. User Rights:

Users may: • update profile information • control communication preferences • request account deletion. Depending on your location, additional rights may apply under local privacy laws including rights to access, correct, delete, or restrict processing of personal information.

9. Age Requirement:

The Service is not intended for individuals under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors.

10. International Data Transfers:

Information may be processed in the United States and other countries where our service providers operate.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes may be communicated through the Service.

12. Account Deletion Request

You can delete your EventsNPeeps account directly in the app:
Click on the Profile icon in the top-right corner → Select My Profile → Delete Account → Read the confirmation message → Click Confirm to permanently delete your account.
If you are unable to access your account, you may request account deletion by emailing support@eventsnpeeps.com from your registered email address.
When your account is deleted, we permanently remove your account profile and associated user data within 30 days, except where retention is required for legal, security, fraud prevention, or payment-related obligations.
For questions about account deletion, contact support@eventsnpeeps.com.
